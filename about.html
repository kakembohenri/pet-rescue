About 4PAWS

FourPaws Uganda stands as a beacon of compassion and dedication in the realm of animal welfare. Established in 2017, the organization has been at the forefront of rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need across Uganda. Their mission extends beyond the physical well-being of animals to fostering a community that values and respects the lives of every creature.
